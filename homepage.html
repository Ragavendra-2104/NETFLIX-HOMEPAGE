New & Popular

moneyheist

"Money Heist" (La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish heist crime drama television series created by Álex Pina. The story follows a criminal mastermind known as "The Professor," who plans the biggest heist in recorded history – to print billions of euros in the Royal Mint of Spain. To carry out this ambitious plan, he recruits eight criminals with unique skills and no personal connections. The series intricately blends the heist with internal conflicts, law enforcement strategies, and emotional backstories, making it a gripping and suspenseful narrative filled with twists and turns.

Sweet Tooth season 3

"Sweet Tooth" (Season 3)continues the journey of Gus, a half-deer, half-boy hybrid, in a post-apocalyptic world where a virus has decimated the human population. As Gus and his fellow hybrids seek safety and answers, they face new dangers and uncover deeper conspiracies about their origins. The season intensifies the clash between hybrids and humans, with emotional stakes and high tension as Gus learns more about his past and the future of his kind.

Bridgerton(Season 3 part-2)

"Bridgerton" is a Netflix Original historical romance series created by Chris Van Dusen and produced by Shonda Rhimes. Set in Regency-era London, it follows the affluent Bridgerton family as they navigate love, society, and scandal. The series combines lush period details with modern sensibilities, featuring diverse characters and contemporary music covers. Each season focuses on a different Bridgerton sibling's romantic entanglements, blending drama, humor, and intrigue. With lavish costumes and sets, "Bridgerton" offers a visually stunning and emotionally engaging experience.

Hit-Man

"Hit Man" is a Netflix Original romantic comedy directed by Richard Linklater. The film stars Glenn Powell as Gary Johnson, a professor who moonlights as a fake hitman. However, things spiral out of control when he gets deeply entangled in his deceptive role. Balancing humor and tension, the movie explores Gary's double life, relationships, and the unexpected complications that arise from his unusual side job. The story offers a blend of romance, comedy, and suspense, making it a highly anticipated release.
